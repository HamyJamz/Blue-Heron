ABOUT

The Blue Heron Restaurant, celebrating over thirty years of casual fine dining at the lake.

In January, 1984 Joseph H. Boer purchased the home of Glen and Alice Wood. The months that followed saw the home transformed. The doors of The Blue Heron Restaurant opened for the first time on July 4th, 1984.

The restaurant is open Tuesday through Saturday. The doors open at 5:00 pm, and seating begins at 5:30 pm upon arrival, no reservations required. The restaurant's atmosphere is casual evening and there is no dress code.

Larger groups may inquire about our two private dining rooms.

We look forward to seeing you!
